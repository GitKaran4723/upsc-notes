The UPSC CSE

The Union Public Service Commission conducts the Civil Services Examination annually to recruit officers for prestigious services like IAS, IPS, IFS, and 20+ other All India Services.

This three-stage examination is considered one of the toughest competitive exams globally, requiring comprehensive knowledge, analytical thinking, and strategic preparation.

Prelims

Objective-type screening test focusing on factual clarity, conceptual understanding, and speed. Tests general knowledge across diverse subjects.

Mains

Descriptive examination testing analytical depth, structured thinking, and articulation skills. Requires comprehensive answer writing practice.

Interview

Personality test evaluating mental alertness, critical thinking, communication skills, and overall suitability for civil services.
