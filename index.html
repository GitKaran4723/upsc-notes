About the UPSC Exam

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) conducts the Civil Services Examination annually to recruit officers for prestigious services like IAS, IPS, IFS, and others.

This portal serves aspirants with resources for both:

  • Prelims – Objective type paper testing factual clarity and conceptual speed.
  • Mains – Descriptive exams focusing on analytical depth, structure, and articulation.

Start with clarity, prepare with discipline, and revise with purpose — that's the UPSC mantra.
